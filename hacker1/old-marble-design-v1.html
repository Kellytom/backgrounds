Design Evolution Notes

Original Design (This File): Used radial gradients positioned at various points to create curved, organic veining patterns that looked natural but didn't have controlled angles.

Failed Design (failed-design.html): Attempted to use linear gradients at specific angles (30-60 degrees) but resulted in harsh, artificial-looking veining that was either too strong or not visible enough.

Current Approach: Focus on striped patterns and other techniques that work better for the intended use cases.
